MaDeleine's Odyssey


About Author:

Next

Madison Earley aka. MaDeleine is a seasoned writer, poet and content creator with a lifelong passion for storytelling and artistic expression. She brings a unique perspective to the world of words and music. Madison Earley embarked on their creative journey at the age of 7, and has since crafted poetry, short stories, and music that resonate with the human experience. This dedication has resulted in over one thousand songs, poems, a compelling screenplay, and an Instagram poetry account that has been steadily growing for the past six years.



Beyond creating original content, Madison has contributed their expertise in copyediting to other written works including a book on women’s infertility as well as their participation in York University’s Literary Journal, Existere. This showcases their interest in fostering literary communities and supporting emerging voices in the world of literature.


At the core of Madison’s creative endeavors is a desire to inspire others with the same passion that fuels their own work. They envision a future where their words and melodies can have an impact on the hearts and minds of those who seek inspiration and a connection to the human experience. Madison Earley further values authenticity, diversity and inclusion, as well as empowerment and healing. Her themes encompass a wide array of topics, including but not limited to trauma, abuse, healing, and religion, often explored through the lens of sci-fi alterations and personification.

Thank you for joining Madison Earley on their creative odyssey! :)

Poetics

I crave to be comfortable in my skin. To know the true meaning of not caring what other people think. I crave to find that piece of me somewhere within. To take the texture of it and smear it on my face like war paint. I crave to fight myself and win. To know how it feels to win a losing battle. I crave to radiate confidence and have it burst open and out from my skin. To touch something and know I've made it beautiful.

-I Want to Touch Myself. 10.08.17. 4:10pm.

"Pumpkin Patch" Screenplay Excerpt:

Scenery is dark, soft music spills out into the street from the restaurant. Monica smokes a cigarette and watches across the street where two figures tussle. One of the figures gets into the car but it is too dark to see who. The other figure looks in Monica’s direction. Monica realizes it is Mike, they lock eyes, and she waves, but he doesn’t wave back. He gets in the black van and drives away.
